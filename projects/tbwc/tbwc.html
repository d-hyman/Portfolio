Project Overview

A fully autonomous vehicle, fit for a teddy bear, equipped with a course-navigation system, automated trash launcher, and user safety system, overseeing chassis design, sensor integration, and Arduino vehicle logic. Designed 2D AutoCAD orthographic models and isometric sketches for center of mass calculations, and implemented with an Arduino Microcontroller and several sensors incuding an ultrasonic sensor. Met all challenges including obstacle navigation, trash launching, and carrying a 4 kg payload up a ramp.
